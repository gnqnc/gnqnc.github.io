Residency

Dr. Nikolskaya completed her residency in Neurology at a highly reputable and rigorous residency program at the University of California San Diego (UCSD).

Fellowship

Dr. Nikolskaya completed an ACGME accredited Clinical Neurophysiology fellowship at Harbor-UCLA. During the fellowship training, she became skilled in electrodiagnostics including electroencephalography (EEG), electromyography (EMG), nerve conduction studies (NCS). During her fellowship, she acquired additional expertise in neuromuscular disorders, pediatric and adult seizures, vagal nerve stimulator programming, and botulinum toxin treatment of various conditions including chronic migraines, cervical dystonia, arm or leg dystonia, blepharospasm, hemifacial spasm.

Clinical Interests

Dr. Nikolskaya has additional experience with headache management, traumatic brain injuries, ADHD and pediatric behavior management.
