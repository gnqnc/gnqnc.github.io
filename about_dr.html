ABOUT DR. GALINA NIKOLSKAYA

Dr. Galina Nikolskaya is a board certified neurologist who completed her residency in neurology at the University of California San Diego followed by clinical neurophysiology fellowship at Harbor-UCLA where she became skilled in electrodiagnostics including electroencephalography, electromyography, nerve conduction studies, acquired additional expertise in neuromuscular disorders, epilepsy, vagal nerve stimulator programming, and botulinum toxin treatment of various neurological conditions. She has an additional interest and experience in the various headache disorders and movement disorders and was trained by accomplished experts in the field providing care at the Headache Center of South California and the UCSD Movement Disorder Center designated as the Center of Excellence.

Dr. Nikoslkaya's knowledgeable and personable approach contributed to her winning recognition during a Spring 2017 HealthTap Best Doctor competition as the Top 1 Neurologist in the Orange Country Region. In her private practice she consistently receives the highest patient satisfaction feedback.

She is passionate about helping patients reach their wellness goals and believes in preventative care. She will coach patients on appropriate health habits, including holistic approaches, and will advise on strategies for wellbeing. She firmly believes in giving the patients as much time as possible to express their concerns.
