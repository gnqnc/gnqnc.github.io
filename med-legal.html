Forensic Neurology

As a neurologist, Dr. Nikolskaya can review records, conduct independent medical evaluations, defense medical evaluations, fitness for duty and personal injury neurological evaluations.

Dr. Nikolskaya can opine and testify on the following areas in her scope of neurology expertise:

  • Standard of care in clinical neurology
  • Causation of central and peripheral nervous system injury (brain injury, spine or nerve injury)
  • Prognosis of patients with neurological problems or neurological injuries
  • Future neurological treatment needs based on the type of neurological injury or condition
  • Neurological medication issues (involving anti-epileptics, migraine, nerve pain medications, anti-psychotics or any other medications used for neurological disorders)

Dr. Nikolskaya has testified in court multiple times as a neurology expert on causation involving traumatic brain injury cases. Her work is known to be of high quality, timely, thorough and precise.
