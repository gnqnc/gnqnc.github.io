Expert Witness

As a neurological expert, Dr. Galina Nikolskaya can review medical records, perform independent medical evaluations, and give opinions or testimony on the following:

  • Standard of care in neurology and if deviations have caused harm to a claimant.
  • Clinical status and prognosis of patients with neurological problems.
  • Neurological medication issues (involving anti-epileptics, migraine, pain medications, botulinum toxin, neuroleptics or any other medications used for neurological disorders).
  • Central and peripheral nervous system injuries.

Dr. Nikolskaya communicates about her opinions promptly with her clients who rely on her services as an expert witness. She has worked with multiple attorneys representing both plaintiffs and defendants including. Dr. Nikolskaya’s work product is known for being timely, precise, and thorough.
