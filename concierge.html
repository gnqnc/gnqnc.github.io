Concierge Neurological Care Services

Dr. Nikolskaya offers a personalized concierge neurological care to a limited number of patients. This service is best suited for patients with chronic neurological conditions requiring frequent monitoring of symptoms, medication adjustment and/or imaging surveillance. Examples of such conditions include parkinson’s disease, migraines, epilepsy, narcolepsy, multiple sclerosis, trigeminal neuralgia, pseudotumor cerebri etc

For potential members, Dr.Nikolskaya offers a free of charge “meet and greet” brief video-session to ensure that she would be an appropriate match for fulfilling these patient’s needs.

The benefits of concierge membership to the patient include:

  • Immediate access to a board certified neurologist’s via cell phone, text messaging and email
  • Expedited referrals to the top specialists in their fields
  • Preventive care counseling - proper diet, fitness habits, sleep habits and cognitive exercises
  • Immediate filling out of any needed forms
  • Unlimited prescription refills and new medications called in

Membership is based on an annual retainer model, where patient pays quarterly, semi-annually or annually to the company. The retainer fee covers most services provided by Dr. Nikolskaya. If patient needs any diagnostics such as lab work, neuropsychiatric testing, imaging, referrals or any other services, the doctor orders these for a patient and patient’s insurance is used to cover their cost.
