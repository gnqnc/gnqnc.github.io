HOW IT WORKS

  • Quick and direct access to a neurologist, patients get seen within 48 hours
  • Neurologist can be reached easily via cell phone or email for any unanswered questions
  • Patients have the option of being evaluated in the comfort of their homes
  • No need to wait for an insurance authorization for a neurology referral or follow-up
  • Office visit can last as long as patient needs
  • Patients can be seen during the regular off hours on late evenings or weekends
  • Full transparency of service fees, patients know exactly how much each service costs
  • Patients know their neurologist is only working for them, without bureaucratic constraints of large medical centers, without restrictions on types of medications, labs, or imaging studies
  • Insurance companies have no influence on how neurologist practices best medical care
  • Patients can use insurance to get the cost of labs and images covered
