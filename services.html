Services

Patients with a wide spectrum of neurological diseases are evaluated and treated including the following but not limited to:

  • Various headaches including migraines, tension, cluster, various cranial neuralgias
  • Chronic migraine patients get botulinum toxin injection treatment every 3 months
  • Occipital neuralgia patients get occipital nerve blocks on as needed basis
  • Movement disorders including Parkinsons, Progressive Supranuclear Palsy, Multiple Systems atrophy, Cervical and task specific dystonias, Meige’s, Blepharospasm, Hemifacial spasm, Essential tremor, Tourette's, Restless Leg syndrome, Stiff person syndrome, Huntington’s disease, various hereditary Ataxias, and Paroxysmal Movement disorders
  • Sleep disorders: insomnia, narcolepsy, circadian rhythm sleep disorder (advanced/delayed sleep phase disorder), periodic limb movement disorder, REM sleep behavior disorder.
  • Strokes and Transient Ischemic Attacks
  • Inflammatory CNS diseases: multiple sclerosis, neuromyelitis optica, transverse myelitis
  • Neuromuscular junction autoimmune diseases: myasthenia gravis, lambert-eaton
  • Inflammatory polyneuropathies: CIDP, Mononeuritis Multiplex, Multifocal acquired demyelinating sensory and motor neuropathy
  • Inflammatory myopathies such as Dermatomyositis, Polymyositis, Statin-Induced -Autoimmune Myopathy, Inclusion Body Myositis, Infectious Myopathies
  • Acquired or hereditary myopathies
  • Hereditary neuropathies:Charcot-Marie-Tooth, Hereditary Neuropathy with Liability to Pressure Palsies
  • Hereditary muscular dystrophies such as Duchenne and Becker muscular dystrophies
  • Local nerve or root entrapments: carpal tunnel, ulnar neuropathy, radiculopathy
  • Epilepsy: focal and generalized
  • Postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome
  • Dementias: Alzheimer's, Frontotemporal, Lewy Body,Vascular, CJD, Wernicke-Korsakoff syndrome, Normal pressure hydrocephalus, Mixed, Huntington’s and Parkinson’s disease
  • Disorders of CSF (cerebrospinal fluid): idiopathic intracranial hypertension (pseudotumor cerebri), normal pressure hydrocephalus, intracranial hypotension (low pressure headache)
  • Degenerative motor neuron disorders: ALS (Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis), PLS (Primary Lateral Sclerosis), Progressive bulbar palsy, Hereditary Spastic Paraplegia
