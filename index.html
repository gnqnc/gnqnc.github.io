Second Opinion Visits

This is a growing phenomenon when a patient goes to see another specialist in the same field for a second opinion. A recent study conducted at Mayo clinic indicated that as many as 88% of patients who came for a 2nd opinion, ended up with an alternative diagnosis, and with this new diagnosis their plan of care was changed and so were their lives.

Neurology with its complexity and confounding factors like polypharmacy, chronic medical problems, occupational injuries, and psychiatric issues, is a field where a misdiagnosis is more likely.

Dr. Nikolskaya offers second opinion visits and hopes to spare patients from unnecessary burden of wrong medications, numerous tests and imaging and increase patient’s quality of life one correct diagnosis at a time.
