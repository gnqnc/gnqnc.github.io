About Dr. Galina Nikolskaya

Dr. Galina Nikolskaya is a double board-certified neurologist (ABPN in Neurology and ABMS in Clinical Neurophysiology) with over 15 years of clinical experience, treating patients with a range of conditions, including neurodegenerative brain decline, brain injury, oculomotor issues, headaches, seizures, movement disorders, and balance issues. In her active clinical private practice in San Diego, she employs advanced neurodiagnostic testing to support patients with brain injuries, blending medical expertise with a compassionate, integrative approach.

Known for her vast fund of knowledge, practical approach, thorough neurodiagnostic evaluations, engaging presentations, and dedication to empowering others, Dr. Nikolskaya thrives on helping professionals and patients optimize their brain function.

Services offered:

  • Neurological Consultations in San Diego, CA
  • Consulting on Brain Wellness and Longevity for High-Achieving Professionals
  • TBI Consulting for Attorneys (including help with screening of the prospective clients)
  • Speaking Engagements on Brain Wellness, Stress Reset, and TBI
  • Clinical Neurophysiology Studies: EMG/NCS, EEG, VNG, and Posturography
  • Chemodenervation treatments with Xeomin for spasticity, migraines, nerve pain, and dystonia

Brain Wellness and Longevity Consulting

She also extends her expertise to high-achieving professionals, serving as a brain wellness and brain optimization consultant for those wanting to take their cognitive performance and body health to the next level. Dr. Nikolskaya translates cutting-edge neuroscience into practical, evidence-based protocols for brain optimization, tailored for high-stress professionals, including trial attorneys who face adrenaline surges, cognitive overload, sleep debt, and decision fatigue. Drawing on thousands of hours of training in psychological modalities (including ACT, CBT, IFS, DBT, EMDR, hypnosis, somatic methods, psychodrama, breathwork, and contemplative practices) she partners with law firms and organizations nationwide to foster sustainable habits that enhance attention, memory, executive function, and overall brain health.
